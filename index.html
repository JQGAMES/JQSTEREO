Artist Statement

With my first album, I explore the realization that in the age of computers, our lives are increasingly controlled, confined, and shaped by forces beyond our awareness. This sense of captivity runs throughout the project, giving space to emotions such as anger, loneliness, and despair, unfiltered and intense.

Nature becomes a crucial anchor within this struggle, a way to reconnect and begin breaking free from this invisible form of enslavement. As the album progresses, this escape becomes more tangible, leading toward a transformation into a new, more positive era defined by peace.

Between loud, aggressive tracks, there are moments of retreat into nature, where everything feels calm and balanced. Dreams also play an important role: they offer hope and reveal that there is a way out. They remind us that freedom is possible, that we can move through the world lightly and freely, like a goat jumping without limits.

Artist Biography

JQ STEREO, born in 1987 in Germany, is an artist driven by a deep curiosity for the complexity of human experience. From an early age, he began exploring music and its emotional impact, regardless of genre, developing a broad and open understanding of sound, expression, and feeling.

Operating under the pseudonym JQ STEREO, he first stepped into the artistic world through music. From the beginning, his creative focus has been clear: language, meaning, and above all, the emotional impact of his work. Rather than prioritizing conventional structures, he aims to capture and convey authentic feelings, creating atmospheres that resonate with listeners on a personal level.

Central to his artistic identity is a strong connection to people. Having always worked closely with others, JQ STEREO draws inspiration from real interactions and shared experiences. He is particularly motivated by the desire to influence people in a positive way whether through music, expression, or the emotions his art evokes.

His work stands as a reflection of both introspection and connection: a continuous attempt to translate thoughts and feelings into something tangible, meaningful, and human.
